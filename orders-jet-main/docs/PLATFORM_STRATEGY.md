### **From Plugin to Platform**
What started as a restaurant management plugin for Orders Jet has evolved into **WooJet** - a comprehensive WooCommerce transformation platform that revolutionizes how businesses manage their operations across multiple industries.

### **The Discovery**
While building the restaurant solution, we discovered something profound: **WooCommerce's default interface is fundamentally broken for modern business operations.** The problems weren't unique to restaurants:
- **Slow Performance**: 50-100 database queries per page load
- **Outdated Interface**: Table-based layouts from 2010
- **Generic Workflows**: One-size-fits-none approach
- **Poor Mobile Experience**: Desktop-focused design
- **No Role-Based Logic**: Everyone sees everything

### **The Innovation**
Our solution went far beyond fixing restaurant management. We created:
- **Smart Card Interface**: Beautiful, functional cards replacing boring table rows
- **High-Performance Architecture**: 80-90% query reduction, sub-1-second load times
- **Role-Based Workflows**: Context-aware interfaces for different user types
- **Customizable Processes**: Statuses, badges, colors, and workflows that match business reality
- **Mobile-First Design**: Interfaces that work how people actually work
